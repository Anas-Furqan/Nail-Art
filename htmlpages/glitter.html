GLITTER DESIGNS

What You Will Need

  • Pink nail polish
  • Metallic green nail polish
  • Makeup sponge
  • Glitter top coat

Step By Step Tutorial

  1. Start with a black base.
  2. Apply some metallic green on a makeup sponge and dab it onto your nail.
  3. Now apply the pink on a makeup sponge and dab onto the nail.

What You Will Need

  • Green nail polish
  • A thin brush

Step By Step Tutorial

What You Will Need

  • Nude-gray nail polish
  • Lavender nail polish
  • Rounded-hole nail art labels

Step By Step Tutorial

  1. For your base, paint your nails with a lavender nail polish.
  2. Once it dries, place two round nail art labels on either side of the nail, leaving a gap at the center.
  3. Allow it to dry and apply a coat of clear polish to finish.

What You Will Need

  • Pale pink nail polish
  • Pastel orange nail polish
  • White nail polish

Step By Step Tutorial

  1. Paint your nails using the pale pink nail polish.
  2. Repeat the process with the white and the orange nail polish.
  3. Apply a coat of clear nail polish to set.
