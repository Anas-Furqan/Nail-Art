FLOWER DESIGNS

What You Will Need

  • A white nail polish
  • Pink nail polish
  • Purple nail polish
  • A thin nail art brush or a nail art pen

Step By Step Tutorial

  • Start by applying two coats of white nail polish for your base.
  • Using a thin brush, create the flowers with a pink nail polish.
  • Use the purple nail polish to add some oomph to the pink flowers.
  • Finish with a top coat.

What You Will Need

  • An angled nail art brush
  • Blue nail polish
  • Purple nail polish
  • Pink nail polish

Step By Step Tutorial

  • Apply two coats of white nail polish for a good base.
  • Once it dries, dip the brush in blue nail polish and using your thumb, splash the color over to your nails.
  • Follow this process for each color until you achieve the look that you want.
  • Apply a top coat to set.

What You Will Need

  • Purple-pink nail polish
  • Yellow nail polish
  • Nail art strips
  • Top coat

Step By Step Tutorial

  • Start by painting your nails yellow.
  • Paint the lower half of the nail with a peach nail polish, going diagonally across the nail.
  • Repeat the same with the purplish-pink nail polish, going diagonally across the other way.
  • To finish, apply a top coat and your'e good to go!

What You Will Need

  • A light-peach nail polish
  • Pink nail polish
  • Toothpicks
  • Sponge

Step By Step Tutorial

  • Apply a coat of the light-peach nail polish.
  • Using a toothpick, slightly blend the colors together.
  • Dab the mixed colors using a sponge.
  • Apply a coat of clear polish to finish the look.
